About The Course

This course aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the business, economic, cost, and technical drivers of the industry, ensuring they grasp the 'why' before delving into the 'how'. The course is designed to go beyond industry certification expectations, focusing on the students' ability to architect cost-effective, scalable, and highly available cloud solutions. It aligns with both business-oriented and technical AWS certifications and offers a 50% discount voucher upon successful completion. Students will learn to differentiate between on-premises and cloud infrastructure, make informed decisions on cloud migration, implement architectural solutions using AWS, and optimize infrastructure using AWS's best practices and managed services. The course involves lectures, hands-on labs, group projects, and a gradual learning approach, requiring 8-16 hours of study and work per week.

Prerequisite & Recommendation

Required: IS52. Recommended: IS181

Course Objectives

Explore the disruptive impact of Cloud Computing as the 3rd wave of computing and its significance in IT infrastructure. Gain a comprehensive understanding of the business, economic, cost, and technical drivers in the IT industry. Learn to architect cost-effective, scalable, and highly available cloud solutions following industry best practices. Align with AWS cloud practitioner and solutions architect-associate certifications, with a 50% discount voucher upon successful completion. Develop the ability to differentiate between on-premises and cloud infrastructure, make informed decisions on cloud migration, and utilize AWS services for scalable, reliable, and highly available infrastructure.
