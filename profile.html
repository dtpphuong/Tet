About The Course

This course aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the business. The course is designed to go beyond industry certification expectations, focusing on the students' ability to architect cost-effective, scalable, and highly available cloud solutions. It aligns with both business-oriented and technical AWS

Course Objectives

Explore the disruptive impact of Cloud Computing as the 3rd wave of computing and its significance in IT infrastructure. Gain a comprehensive understanding of the business, economic, cost, and technical drivers in the IT industry. Learn to architect cost-effective, scalable, and highly available cloud solutions following industry best practices.
